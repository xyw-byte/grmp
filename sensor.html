Motion Sensor Control Technology 人体感应控制技术

Intelligent LED control based on human presence detection 基于人体存在检测的智能LED控制方案

Motion sensor technology enables automatic LED lighting control by detecting human movement and presence. Our solutions integrate PIR (Passive Infrared) and microwave sensing technologies, providing high accuracy, low power consumption, and energy-saving benefits for both residential and commercial applications.

人体感应技术通过检测人体活动与存在，实现LED照明的自动控制。我们的方案融合了被动红外（PIR）与微波感应技术，具备高检测精度、低功耗特性，为住宅与商业场景提供节能高效的照明控制方案。

Key Features 核心特性

Dual-tech Sensing
双技术感应
Combines PIR (heat detection) and microwave (motion detection) to reduce false triggers from pets, sunlight, and environmental interference.
融合被动红外（热量检测）与微波（运动检测）技术，减少宠物、阳光及环境干扰导致的误触发。
Energy Saving
节能高效
Automatic on/off control based on human presence, reducing energy consumption by 60%-80% compared to manual control.
根据人体存在自动开关控制，相比手动控制降低60%-80%能耗。
Full Parameter Adjustment
全参数可调

检测距离、延时时间、光照灵敏度全参数可调，适配不同应用场景与用户需求。
