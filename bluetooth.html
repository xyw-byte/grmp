Bluetooth Control Technology 蓝牙控制技术

Wireless control solutions with low power consumption and easy pairing 低功耗、易配对的无线控制解决方案

Bluetooth technology provides reliable short-to-medium range wireless connectivity for LED control systems. Our advanced Bluetooth solutions support BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) for extended battery life, mesh networking for scalable installations, and seamless integration with mobile devices.

蓝牙技术为LED控制系统提供可靠的中短距离无线连接。我们先进的蓝牙解决方案支持BLE（低功耗蓝牙）以延长电池寿命，支持Mesh网络以实现可扩展安装，并能与移动设备无缝集成。
