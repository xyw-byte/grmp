Touch Technology Types 触摸技术类型

Capacitive Touch
电容式触摸
Detects changes in electrical charge from human contact, offering high sensitivity and durability with no moving parts.
通过检测人体接触引起的电荷变化来工作，具有高灵敏度和耐用性，无活动部件。
Resistive Touch
电阻式触摸
Works through pressure applied to a flexible surface, ideal for harsh environments and operation with gloves.
通过施加在柔性表面上的压力工作，适用于恶劣环境和戴手套操作的场景。
Surface Acoustic Wave
表面声波式
Uses ultrasonic waves on glass surface for high clarity and precision, resistant to contaminants.
利用玻璃表面的超声波实现高清晰度和精度，抗污染能力强。
Infrared Touch
红外触摸
Detects interruptions in infrared light grid, supporting multi-touch with excellent durability and optical clarity.
通过检测红外光网格的中断来工作，支持多点触摸，具有出色的耐用性和光学清晰度。
