Button control technology provides intuitive, tactile interaction for LED lighting systems. Our solutions include momentary, toggle, and touch-sensitive buttons with various interface options, offering reliable operation in harsh environments with long service life and customizable functionality.

按键控制技术为LED照明系统提供直观的触觉交互方式。我们的解决方案包括瞬时型、切换型和触摸感应型按键，提供多种接口选项，在恶劣环境中仍能可靠运行，具有长使用寿命和可定制功能。

Button Types 按键类型

Momentary Buttons
瞬时按键
Make contact only when pressed, returning to normal state when released - ideal for temporary functions.
仅在按下时接通，释放后返回正常状态 - 适用于临时功能。
Toggle Switches
切换开关
Maintain their state after being pressed, toggling between on and off positions with each operation.
按下后保持其状态，每次操作在开和关位置之间切换。
Touch Sensors
触摸传感器
Detect capacitance changes from human touch, offering sealed design with no moving parts.
检测人体触摸引起的电容变化，提供无活动部件的密封设计。
Rotary Encoders
旋转编码器
Provide incremental control through rotation, ideal for dimming functions and menu navigation.
通过旋转提供增量控制，适用于调光功能和菜单导航。
